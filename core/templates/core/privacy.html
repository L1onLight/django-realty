Privacy Policy

Voluntary Information Collection
We collect personal information from you when you voluntarily provide it to us. This may include your name, contact details, email address, and any other information you choose to share with us. We use this information to provide you with the services you request, such as property listings, real estate advice, or property valuation. We may also use your personal information to communicate with you, respond to your inquiries, and send you relevant information about properties that match your preferences.
Safeguarding Your Personal Information
At Stellar Realty Agency, we understand the importance of safeguarding your personal information. We have implemented industry-standard security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our website is encrypted to ensure the secure transmission of your information, and we regularly update our security protocols to stay ahead of emerging threats.
Trusted Partners
We may share your personal information with trusted third parties who assist us in providing our services. These third parties may include property appraisers, legal advisors, lenders, or other real estate professionals involved in your property transactions. However, we only disclose the necessary information required for them to perform their respective services, and we ensure that they have adequate safeguards in place to protect your data.
Legal Obligations and Business Transfers
We may also disclose your personal information if required by law or in response to a valid legal request, such as a court order or government inquiry. Additionally, in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of our business assets, your personal information may be transferred to the relevant parties involved.
Respecting Your Privacy
Stellar Realty Agency does not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. We may, however, use non-personal information, such as statistical data or aggregated demographics, for marketing and research purposes.
Privacy Matters
In conclusion, your privacy is of utmost importance to us at Stellar Realty Agency. We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information and handle it responsibly. By using our services, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us.
